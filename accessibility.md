  <p class="mono-kicker">Accessibility</p>
  <h1>Accessibility Statement</h1>
  <p>Ben Chan Tech LLC aims for YY and Me to be usable by visitors with different devices, input methods, and accessibility needs.</p>
  <h2>Current approach</h2>
  <p>The site uses semantic HTML, keyboard-focusable links and controls, visible text links, responsive layouts, and RSS/audio alternatives where practical.</p>
  <h2>Ongoing work</h2>
  <p>Accessibility is ongoing. Older audio, transcripts, embeds, and third-party podcast platforms may have limitations outside direct site control.</p>
  <h2>Contact</h2>
  <p>To report an accessibility issue, email <a href="mailto:ben@benchantech.com">ben@benchantech.com</a> with the page URL, device/browser, and a short description.</p>
